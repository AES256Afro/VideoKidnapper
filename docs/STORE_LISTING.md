## Full description (Store "description")

VideoKidnapper pulls videos off the web, cuts them down, and exports clean GIFs or MP4s — entirely on your own machine. Nothing uploads, nothing is watermarked, and there's no account to make.

**Download from the web**
Paste a link from YouTube, Instagram, X, Reddit, Bluesky, or Facebook — plus 1,000+ other sites yt-dlp supports. Ctrl+V a link from anywhere in the app to start. Queue a batch of links and grab them in one go. Reads your browser cookies when a private or age-gated video needs a login.

**Trim to the exact moment**
Frame-accurate timeline with a waveform and thumbnail strip. Queue several cuts from one video and export them separately or stitched together with transitions.

**Captions that look right**
Add text with outline, shadow, bold/italic, and multiple lines — the preview matches the exported frame exactly, so no guessing. Auto-caption speech with Whisper, or drop in an .srt/.vtt file and get time-synced layers.

**Overlays**
Drop logos, watermarks, and sticker or GIF overlays anywhere on the frame, with per-layer size, opacity, and timing. Paste an image straight from the clipboard.

**Export that fits the platform**
Tune GIFs the way you want (dither, palette, loop count) or export MP4s with hardware encoding when your GPU supports it. Reframe 16:9 into 9:16 for Shorts, Reels, and TikTok with a blurred-background fill instead of ugly bars. Speed, rotate, mute, audio-only, and colour adjustments all built in.

**Also**
Record your screen straight into the editor. Free and open source. FFmpeg is included.
